Change mailing instructions

Use this form to stop receiving statements, remittance vouchers, envelopes or returns by mail.

Copies of notices

Use this form if you want to request a copy of notices previously issued within the last ten years to your account.

Customized statements

Use this form if you want to request a statement of arrears and/or a statement of interim payments for a time frame of your choice.

Additional remittance vouchers

Use this form if you want to request additional remittance vouchers.

Interest review

Use this form to request an interest review for your account or to request a statement of interest for a specified period for your account.

Payment search

Use this form when you want to request a search for a payment that has been made but has not been credited to your account.

Refund

Use this form if you want to request a refund of an excess credit amount from your account

Transfer of credit

Use this form to request the transfer of a credit within this program account. From this program account to a different program account, or between accounts.

Submit a written enquiry

If you cannot get an answer to your question through one of the above online requests, use this form to submit an enquiry.

After receiving your enquiry, we may contact you by telephone for more information. We will provide an electronic response in the View mail service in My Business Account. If you choose to be notified by email when your response is available, we will send you an email when your response is ready to view.
