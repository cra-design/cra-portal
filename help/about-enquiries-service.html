CRA account help - Businesses

About Enquiries service – CRA account help - Businesses

Before calling our business enquiries line for assistance, you can complete submit common requests and written enquiries in writing online.

Purpose of common request web forms

Find out when to use each request web form:

Transfer of a credit
Either within this account or between other accounts
Request a refund
For excess credit amounts from your account
Request a payment search
For a payment that has been made but has not been credited to your account
Request customized statements
Statement of arrears or of interim payments, for a time frame of your choice
Request copies of notices
For notices previously issued within the last ten years to your account
Change mailing instructions
To stop or start receiving statements, remittance vouchers, envelopes or returns by mail
Request interest review or statement of interest
For an interest review, a statement of interest for a specified period, or both for your account
Request remittance vouchers
For additional remittance vouchers

Reporting and tax options (elections)

Several reporting and tax options are called elections, including reporting periods, fiscal year dates, accounting methods and other tax options.

To change these tax options, you must File an election.

  1. In the left menu, select your RT number under GST/HST to view your GST/HST program account overview
  2. Select the tab Reporting periods and other tax options (called elections) to view your election summary
  3. Select the button File an election
  4. Select an election web form option or select Other to upload an eligible completed PDF form
    • For more information on available web forms, select Help

Submiting a written enquiry

If you cannot get an answer to your question through one of the above online requests, use this form to submit an enquiry.

After receiving your enquiry, we may contact you by telephone for more information. We will provide an electronic response in the View mail service in My Business Account. If you choose to be notified by email when your response is available, we will send you an email when your response is ready to view.
