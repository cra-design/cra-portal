Adjusting a GST/HST return in your Business account – CRA account help

You may be looking for:

  • File your GST/HST return
  • Instructions for preparing a GST/HST return
 

The "Adjust a return" web form within your Business account allows you to make changes some previously filed GST/HST returns.

On this page

  • Returns you can adjust
  • Returns you cannot adjust
  • Adjusting rebate amounts (line numbers)
  • Adding a schedule
  • Sales and other revenue
  • Calculation of ITC recapture
  • Reconciliation of Recaptured Input Tax Credits (RITCs)

Returns you can adjust

The returns eligible for electronic adjustment will be listed by period end date in the drop-down menu at the top of the form.

The following previously filed returns can be adjusted in your CRA business account:

  • Form GST34, Goods and Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax Return for Registrants
  • Form RC7200, GST/HST and QST Return for Selected Listed Financial Institutions

To adjust another type of return (such as Form GST106, Information on Claims Paid or Credited for Foreign Conventions and Tour Packages), send your request in writing to your tax centre.

Returns you cannot adjust

A return is not eligible for electronic adjustment if any of the following conditions apply:

  • The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) has not finished processing the original return.
  • The CRA has not received or processed the full payment (if applicable) for the original return. The return will be available for electronic adjustment when the full payment has been applied to the account; or
    • If a monthly or quarterly return: 14 days have passed from either the date the return was received or the due date of the return, whichever is later.
    • If an annual return: 14 days have passed from the date the return was received.
  • The reporting period end date is over four years old. Requests to adjust a period over four years old must be sent in writing to your tax centre.
  • The reporting period is or was subject to an audit or appeals review. Requests to adjust a period subject to one of these reviews must be sent in writing to your tax centre.

Adjusting rebate amounts (line numbers)

Public Service Bodies' (PSB) rebates can be adjusted using the Adjust a PSB rebate service. All other rebate information cannot be adjusted electronically, including the following line numbers:
  • Line 111 - Total rebates
  • Line 122 - BC transition rebate
  • Line 211
  • Line 1300 - Provincial transitional new housing rebates assigned to you by purchasers
  • Line 1301 - Other rebates
  • Line 1402R

You can adjust these other amounts in writing, by sending your request to the Atlantic or Sudbury Tax Centre. For more information on GST/HST rebates, visit Claim a GST/HST rebate.

Adding a schedule

Select Schedule A and/or Schedule B if you did not file the schedule with your original return and you want to add this information as part of your adjustment request.

Sales and other revenue

Select this checkbox if you did not provide a breakdown of your sales and other revenue on your original return and you want to provide this information as part of your adjustment request.

Calculation of ITC recapture

On line 1401, enter the total amount of input tax credits for the provincial portion of the HST on specified property or services that are subject to recapture. Line 1402 (Net RITCs) will be calculated when you select the Next button.

For businesses that have elected to use the estimation/reconciliation method of reporting recaptured input tax credits, line 1401 cannot be adjusted electronically if the applicable fiscal year has already been reconciled. To adjust this amount, contact your tax centre.

Reconciliation of Recaptured Input Tax Credits (RITCs)

On line 1402A, enter the actual amount of Net RITCs for the fiscal year being reconciled. This would be determined by reviewing your financial records at the end of the fiscal year.

Line 1402R cannot be adjusted electronically.

The Adjustment to Net Tax fields will be calculated when you select the Next button.
