Moving within a form

  • Enter the appropriate data in each box or field.
  • To move from one field to the next, use the Tab key. This helps to ensure that you donâ€™t accidentally skip a field.
  • You can also use your mouse to move from field to field. Place your cursor in the field you want to fill in, then left-click.

Changing your data

  • To replace data you've already entered in a field, place your cursor within the field and then double-click. This selects the data, which you can then adjust or replace.

Multi-line fields

  • Some fields have room for more than one line of text. Keep typing when you are at the end of a line and your text will automatically wrap to the next line.
  • If you want to start typing on a new line, press the Enter key.
  • When you've finished in a field, press the tab key to move to the next field.

Radio buttons

  • To select a radio button, place your cursor in the radio button and left click.
  • If your selection is incorrect, left click in the radio button you want.

Check boxes

  • To make an entry in a box, place your cursor in the box and left-click.
  • To remove an entry from a box, place your cursor in the box and left-click.

Pick-list features

  • For some questions, you will have to select from a list of choices.
  • You may make your selection by using the arrow keys or the mouse to move up or down a list to highlight your choice.
  • Then, select by pressing Enter or by clicking on your choice using the left mouse button.
