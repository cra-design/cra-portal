When you have more than one business

After you have entered one or more nine-digit Business Numbers (BN) into the Manage my profile page, they will be available in the drop-down box on the Welcome page. This will allow you to select the BN you want to transact for.

The account information available from the Welcome page will correspond to the BN you select.

For example, you have two businesses that each have a payroll account. If you select the BN for your first business, you will be able to see the payroll information for that business by selecting any of the services available under the Payroll heading. If you select the BN for the second business, all the payroll information available will apply to the second business.

Note: If you remove all the BNs from the Manage my profile page, a BN will no longer appear on the Welcome page and no services will be available. To regain these services, you will need to add a Business Number by selecting the Manage my profile link.

Choosing a default Business Number

If you have more than one business, choose the one that you will want to view most often.

What you can update in your profile

You can update the information found in your profile by selecting the links available for each information type.

Addresses

Select Manage addresses to change a business address.

To learn more about different types of business addresses, refer to Change of business address.

Direct deposit

Select Manage direct deposits to start, update, or stop direct deposit for one or more of your business accounts.

Program account names

Select Manage program account names to edit or delete program account names.

Operating names

Select Manage operating names to change, add, or delete an operating name.

To learn more, refer to Change a business operating name.

Phone numbers

Select Manage phone numbers to update owner phone numbers.

Notification preferences (email)

Select Manage notification preferences to add or remove email addresses to receive notifications when eligible correspondence is available in your account or important changes are made on your account.

Mail delivery method

Select Manage mail for my business to manage your CRA business mail delivery method (online or paper).

Authorized representatives

Select Deleted/expired representative list to view deleted and expired representatives that were acting on your behalf during the last 365 days.

Select Confirm pending requests to confirm or deny electronic authorization requests submitted by representatives. You must confirm or deny requests within 10 business days or the request will be cancelled.

To learn more, refer to Confirm my representative’s authorization request.  

Select View authorized representatives to view representatives currently authorized to act on your behalf, add a new representative, or cancel authorization.

Business numbers

Select Manage business number(s) to add or remove a business from your account.

If you have more than one business, choose the one that you will want to view most often to set as the default.

Language preferences

Select Manage language preferences to choose between English and French for each program account.

Security settings

The following settings affect your CRA account overall and are not specific to My Business Account:

  • CRA user ID, password, security questions and more
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Personal Identification Number (PIN)

To update these security settings:

  1. Select Continue to settings to access the welcome page
  2. Select Security settings (gears icon) on the top right below the page title
  3. Select the Continue button for the setting you want to update

Changes outside of your profile

To notify us of changes related to growing your business or changes to business operations, visit Maintain your business.

To change certain GST/HST-related tax and reporting options, you may need to file an election. To view your current elections in My Business Account, select your GST/HST account, then select the Elections tab.
