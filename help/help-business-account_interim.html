CRA account help - Businesses

My Business Account overview – CRA account help - Businesses

The overview page primarily enables you to access instances of program account types (such as GST/HST, payroll or corporation income tax, etc.) associated with every Business Number (BN) added to My Business Account. Outstanding returns and payments are highlighted for your quick reference. You can also view the status of any in-progess or recently completed submitions in the progress tracker.

On this page

  • Checking your business name and BN
  • If you have more than one business
  • Access all your program account types
  • If you have multiples of a program account type

Checking your business name and BN

The legal name registered on your BN registration records and the nine-digit BN are both displayed at the top-right corner of the overview page. For example:

ABC Industries Inc.
123456789

Your BN is also displayed at the top of the left-menu in the Business number (BN) drop-down.

If you have more than one business

If you have more than one BN added to your account, you can select another BN from the drop-down options at the top of the left menu.

You can add BNs or choose which BN is displayed by default from the Profile page you can find in the left menu. For more details, refer to the help page About your profile.

Access all your program account types

The overview page displays the following types of accounts, if they are associated with your BN:

  • Air Travellers Security Charge (RG)
  • Compliance and Exchange (RZ)
  • Contract Payments (RZ)
  • Corporation Income Tax (RC)
  • COVID-19 rent subsidies (ZA)
  • Digital services tax (DT)
  • Excise duty (RD)
  • Excise tax (RE)
  • Excise tax on insurance premiums (RN)
  • Fuel charge (CT)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Information Returns (RZ)
  • Luxury tax (LT)
  • Partnerships (RZ)
  • Payroll (RP)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • TFSA (RZ)
  • Underused housing tax (RU)

You can access each account from 2 locations on the overview page.

Center column

In the center column of the page, in the accounts section, a panel is displayed for each program account type associated with your BN. You can click on the account link to view more information. For example, RT0001 for GST/HST or RP0001 for your Payroll.

Left menu

In the left menu, all program account types associated with your BN are listed. Each one expands to list instances of this program account type. For example, RT0001 for your GST/HST account.

Select the expanded instances to view more information.

If you have multiples of a program account type

In the left menu, you will find all your program account types in drop-down list. When you expand one, all instances of this program account type are displayed where you can select which one you want to view or action:

For example, if your business has two GST/HST accounts:

  • GST/HST
    • RT0001
    • RT0002

Select either one to display the information associated with that account in My Business Account.
