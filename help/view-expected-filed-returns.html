CRA account help - Businesses

View expected and filed returns – CRA account help - Businesses

You can view a list of expected returns and their due dates in your Business account, as well as detailed information about your filed returns.

Expected returns

The expected returns table lists the returns that are expected by the CRA, and their respective due dates.

The "Status" column will display one of the following:

  • Return overdue: Means that your return is late.
  • Expected return: Means that your return is expected but is not overdue.
  • Next return: Indicates the return that will be due next.

Filed returns

The filed returns table displays information for returns covering the last six years. If a return has been reassessed, the most recent reassessment will be displayed first.

The "Status" column will display one of the following:

  • Received - Processing in progress: Means we have received your return, but have not yet processed it.
  • Assessed: Means we have processed your return, and a Notice of Assessment has been mailed.
  • Processed - No notice: Means we have processed your return, and a Notice of Assessment was not mailed.
  • Adjustment requested: Means we have received your adjustment request, but have not yet processed it.
  • Reassessed: Means we have changed your assessment, and a Notice of Reassessment has been mailed.
  • Adjusted - No notice: Means we have processed your adjustment request, and a Notice of Assessment was not mailed.
  • Adjustment denied: Means we have denied your adjustment request.

You can view detailed line-by-line information of a GST34, GST494, RC7200 or RC7294 return by selecting the hyperlink in the «Reporting Period» column.

Note: this service is not available for Form GST106, Information on Claims Paid or Credited for Foreign Conventions and Tour Packages or Form GST111, Financial Institution GST/HST Annual Information Return.
