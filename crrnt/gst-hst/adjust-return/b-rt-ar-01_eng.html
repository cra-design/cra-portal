Confirmation of receipt

After you successfully submit your request, you will receive a confirmation number. This confirmation number is your proof that the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) has received your request. Print the page with your confirmation number and keep it with your records.

After we've processed your request, we may have to contact you for more information. Keep all supporting documents for six years. This applies to all tax returns, whether you file them on paper or electronically.
